Does Dental PanoAI replace the dentist's diagnostic judgment?+

No â€” and we are explicit about this. Dental PanoAI is a clinical decision support tool. It surfaces findings, provides confidence scores, and generates a structured report. The treating dentist reviews every finding before it becomes part of the clinical record. The AI provides a second read, not a final diagnosis.

Which radiograph types and formats are supported?+

Dental PanoAI supports panoramic radiographs, periapical films, bitewing series, and CBCT volumes. Files can be uploaded via DICOM, JPEG, or PNG. CBCT analysis generates a 3D finding report with slice-level annotations. Integration with major PACS and dental practice management systems is available via API.

How long does integration with our practice management system take?+

For systems with available APIs (Carestream, Dentsply Sirona, Planmeca, Apteryx, and others), integration is typically complete within 2â€“3 days. PrivaSure also offers a standalone web portal for practices that prefer to upload manually and receive reports via the web interface without a PMS integration.

What is the detection accuracy for early-stage caries?+

In our clinical validation study across 14,000 radiographs, Dental PanoAI achieved 96.4% sensitivity and 94.1% specificity for interproximal caries detection at ICDAS score 2 and above. For periapical pathology, sensitivity was 97.8%. Full validation data and methodology are available in our clinical white paper upon request.

Is patient data retained after analysis?+

No. By default, uploaded radiographs are processed in memory and deleted within 60 minutes of report generation. No images are retained on SynapseHealthTech servers beyond the session. Customers with self-hosted deployments retain full data sovereignty. We do not use customer data to train or update models without explicit written agreement.
