"We recovered AED 7.8 million in previously written-off denials in six months. Our clean claims rate went from 71% to 96% in twelve weeks. The Prior Auth Agent alone freed four full-time staff positions."

Aster DM Healthcare deployed Agentic RCM across 18 UAE facilities in a 10-week rollout. The five-agent pipeline automated patient intake, eligibility verification, prior authorisation, clinical documentation coding, and claim submission. Prior authorisation processing — previously the largest manual bottleneck — became 94% touchless. The Denial Management Agent recovered AED 7.8M in previously denied claims in the first six months, against an implementation cost recovered in under 4 months.
